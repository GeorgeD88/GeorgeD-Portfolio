SpotifyTrees

SpotifyTrees is a tool written in Python that leverages the Spotify API. It makes it easy to organize Spotify playlists that you made as subsets of each other, so it becomes a giant tree of playlists. For example, I use it to organize the many genres and subgenres of EDM playlists I have, which is a tree of about 80 playlists!

Kronos

Kronos is a desktop app written in Python that I planned and developed with a group during a hackathon, all in just 36 hours. It helps users allocate their time better and evaluate what went right or wrong in their planning. Full DevPost here.

Artist-Recommendations

Artist Recommendations was a quick 2 hour speed code I did as a fun challenge for myself. Given some artists, it will grab each artist's top 3 songs and 3 random songs, and then creates a playlist out of the pulled songs.
